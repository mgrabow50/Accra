PARTS HANDLERS

The Accra-Wire LOT CONTROL or PARTS HANDLER will work with your production equipment to greatly increase efficiency and cost effectiveness, while improving quality control. Here are a few of the ways you will be able to improve your bottom line:

Introducing our NEW Bin Destacker!

 

AWC's Bin Destacker is designed as a simple means of effectively keeping tighter control over lot sizes without adding expensive labor. A bin will automatically drop based on a time or piece count ( from the production machine). Unique bin designs and custom configurations for in-house containers are available. The AWC Bin Destacker makes it easier for an operator to run multiple lines more efficiently.

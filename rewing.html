COMPUTER CONTROLLED PRECISION LEVEL LAYER REWIND SYSTEMS

For precise computer control in handling wire, you may need a rewind system with an optional capstan.

The Accra-Wire Controls computer controlled precision level layer rewind system with a motorized take up capstan offers the greatest degree of control for handling bulk wire that needs distortion-free transfer to a variety of spools. Level layer rewind gives a clean presentation for critical applications. Various capstans are available to accommodate precise feed and tensioning requirements.
     
Precision level layer rewind system with optional capstan     Smaller gauges of wire can also be handled precisely with or without a capstan

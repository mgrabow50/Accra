ACCRA-BOTIC DEREELER MODELS D-4 & D-5

Accra-Botic Dereelers Models D-4 and D-5 are heavy duty motor driven dereelers that act like robots. The Model D-5 uses only one pulley. By maintaining slight tension on the wire, the motorized dereelers let you feed aluminium, stainlesss, flux-cored and all diameters of standard wire. These units are designed to pay off bulk wire from 250 to 1,000 lb. spools. The Models D-4 & D-5 can be wired for reversing capabilities (optional) to wind wire back on spools for traversing applications. Modifications of the units to handle different spool sizes and speeds can be obtained by consulting the Accra-Wire Controls factory.

ACCRA-MATIC DEREELER MODEL D-1
 
A heavy duty mechanical payoff stand
Accra-Matic dereeler Model D-1 is a heavy-duty mechanical payoff stand with a special low back tension braking device for paying off wire in bulk form. This stand pays off wire slow or fast with 3 to 7 lbs. pulling force depending on feed speed. 250 to 1,000 lbs. spools standard. The unit is equipped for 30" diameter spools - 22" or 26" spools can be accomodated by using custom ramp.
 
BENEFITS AND COST SAVINGS
- Uses bulk wire - fewer changes needed
- High speed efficiency - wire speed from a   few IPM to 2,000 IPM
- Minimum down time - no forklift needed.   Spools load upright into place in seconds
- Saves space - only 18" wide - most compact   on market with fastest stopping time
- On-the-job versatility - different pulleys and   tensioning devices can be installed on the   stand to run different diameter wires

ACCRA-BOTIC DEREELER MODEL D-12
 
Eliminates pull on wire - at any distance

Accra-Botic Dereeler Model D-12 practically eliminates pull on weld wire. This means dereelers can be set up anywhere, at any convenient distance from the welding operation, and the wire will not flip, twist, stretch, knurl, or slip.

The Model D-12 is so compact that you can install any number of dereelers needed in a central location or right up next to your robot.

Virtually maintenance-free, the D-12 also sets up quickly and easily, needing no adjustments for different sizes of wire or operating speeds. The electronically controlled motor automatically keeps up with the speed of a robot, automatic welder, or even hand welder. The dereeler accepts solid, flux-cored, aluminium and stainless steel wire in all gauges and spool sizes.

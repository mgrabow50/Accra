Weld Wire Dereelers

The Model D-5 uses only one pulley. By maintaining slight tension on the wire, the motorized dereelers let you feed aluminium, stainlesss, flux-cored and all diameters of standard wire. These units are designed to pay off bulk wire from 250 to 1,000 lb. spools. The Models D-4 & D-5 can be wired for reversing capabilities (optional) to wind wire back on spools for traversing applications.
